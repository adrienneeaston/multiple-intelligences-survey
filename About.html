Adrienne Easton

Adrienne Easton is a JavaScript Software Developer who has also worked as school psychologist, psychometrist, and educational specialist. She came up with the idea for this project having given this informal survey to many students over the years to help empower they, their parents, and their teachers to undertand and embrace their strengths, beyond just formal IQ and academic testing. Besides writing code and administering psychoeducational and neuropsychological test, Adrienne also enjoys acting, singing, swimming, and making balloon animals.
